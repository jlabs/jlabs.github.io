ComiCoffee is a hybrid coffee house/comic store offering a warm, inviting space to hang out, drink speciality beverages and read your favourite comics or novels.

We are a proud independent coffee house that serves great coffee made by talented baristas, in a relaxed and welcoming environment where your time is your own.

The first coffee shop on The Isle of Wight that combines the Comic & Pop culture with a hospitality outlet. Selling a range of memorabilia as well as a fantastic range of freshly made products.
